Arammu: The Relationship Checkup™

The Relationship Checkup™ is based on 20 years of federally funded clinical research guiding it towards our goal of successfully intervening early in relationships to give couples tools and techniques to help prevent deterioration and manage their stressors in a healthy way, together. Randomized clinical trials have demonstrated the efficacy of the Checkup for improving relationship health, reducing depressive symptoms, and diminishing the incidence of divorce.
